Who are we?

Founded in 2024, by Ray Carr, AZoom Car Rental© is Singapore's leading electric car rental service, catering to over 50,000 customers nationwide. we offer a diverse fleet of vehicles tailored to suit any journey. Whether you're looking for luxury for a special occasion, premium cars for business, or standard models for everyday use, we have options to meet your needs. We take pride in our excellent customer service and strive to provide a seamless experience with convenient booking, flexible terms, and 24/7 support. Discover the freedom and comfort of driving with AZoom Car Rental© — where your journey begins!

Our Mission

Our mission is to make car rental easy, affordable, and accessible for everyone. At AZoom Car Rental©, we are committed to providing a hassle-free rental experience with a focus on customer satisfaction and convenience. We believe in offering quality vehicles at competitive prices, ensuring that each customer can find the perfect car to suit their needs — whether for work, travel, or special occasions.

Our commitment to you.

At AZoom Car Rental©, safety and quality are our top priorities. We ensure every vehicle in our fleet is rigorously inspected and maintained to the highest standards before each rental. Our team of experienced technicians follows a comprehensive maintenance schedule, addressing everything from routine checks to detailed inspections. By upholding these standards, we aim to provide our customers with a reliable, worry-free driving experience every time they choose AZoom©.
